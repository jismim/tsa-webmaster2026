Welcome to CareMap Morris

This site helps Morris County residents find support and helps neighbors give back to their community.

How to search

  • Use the search bar to look up a service — try "food pantry," "shelter," or "housing."
  • Or open the Directory to browse all categories.

What filters do

  • Type: food pantry, shelter, domestic violence support, homeless services
  • Services: meals, counseling, emergency housing, legal support
  • Give back: donation needs and volunteer roles

Find help vs. give back

  • Find Help: Directory → "Services Provided"
  • Give Back: Directory → "Donation Needs" or "Volunteer Opportunities"
