Welcome to CareMap Morris

This site helps Morris County residents find support and helps neighbors give back.

How to search

  • Use the search bar to look up a service (e.g., “food pantry”, “shelter”, “housing”).
  • Or open the Directory to browse categories.

What filters do

  • Type: food pantry, women’s shelter, domestic violence support, homeless services
  • Services: meals, counseling, emergency housing, legal support
  • Give back: donation needs + volunteer roles

Where to get help vs give back

  • Find Help: Directory → choose “Services Provided”
  • Give Back: Directory → “Donation Needs” or “Volunteer Opportunities”
